Fan Page: One Sound Music

One Sound Music is a collective group of artist whose goal is to provide stories about Jesus through song. I have gathered information about the three co-founders and included it below.

Biographies

Akil Henry: Musician, data analyst & podcaster. Whilst completing a degree in Mathematics and Music at the University of Birmingham, he started One Sound Music with some friends with the ambition to give platforms to Adventist artists, who typically haven’t been part of the gospel/Christian music mainstream market.

Tochi Anu: Guitar, choral direction, songwriting, photography - Tochi’s a true creative to his core.

Antonia Gregory: Like the other team members, Antonia has an extremely diverse musical background, from singing background vocals for Jahmene Douglas (who had a UK no 1 album a few years back), to sound engineering (which she is finishing up a degree in at the moment from the University of West London). Years of singing in church and an innate musical ability have allowed her to develop a keen ear, which she uses to her advantage as a sound engineer and as a vocalist.
